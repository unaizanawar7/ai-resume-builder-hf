## Building Your Resume

### Step 1: Choose Your Input Method

You have two options:

#### Option A: Upload Existing Resume
**Best for:** People who already have a resume

**How it works:**
1. Click the "Upload Resume" card
2. Select your PDF or DOCX file (max 10MB)
3. Wait 5-10 seconds while AI extracts your information
4. Review the extracted data - AI captures:
   - Personal information (name, email, phone, location)
   - Work experience (all positions with descriptions)
   - Education (degrees, institutions, dates)
   - Skills (technical and soft skills)
   - Certifications, projects, and other sections

**Tips:**
- Use a clean, text-based resume for best results
- Avoid image-heavy or overly designed resumes
- PDFs generally parse better than DOCX files

#### Option B: Start From Scratch
**Best for:** First-time resume builders or major career changes

**How it works:**
1. Click "Start From Scratch"
2. Fill in the form with your information:
   - **Personal Information** (required): Name, email
   - **Personal Information** (optional): Phone, location, LinkedIn, GitHub
   - **Professional Summary**: 2-3 sentence overview of your experience
   - **Skills**: Add skills one by one (press Enter or click Add)
   - **Experience**: Add later in the form
   - **Education**: Add later in the form

**Tips:**
- Fill in at least name and email to proceed
- You can always edit information later
- Don't worry about perfection - AI will help polish it

### Step 2: Review and Edit Information

**What you'll see:**
- All extracted or entered information displayed in an organized form
- Sections for Personal Info, Experience, Education, and Skills
- Edit buttons for each field

**What to do:**
1. **Check Personal Information**: Verify name, contact details are correct
2. **Review Experience**: 
   - Look at job titles and companies
   - Check date ranges
   - Verify bullet points accurately represent your work
3. **Verify Education**: 
   - Confirm degree names and institutions
   - Check graduation dates
4. **Confirm Skills**: 
   - Remove any incorrect skills
   - Add any missing skills

**Important:** This is your last chance to ensure information accuracy before AI enhancement!

Click "Continue" when satisfied.

### Step 4: Generate Enhanced Resume

**What happens here:**
The AI reads your information and transforms it into polished, professional content.

**Before generation:**
- **Target Job Role** (Optional but recommended): e.g., "Senior Software Engineer", "Marketing Manager"
- **Target Industry** (Optional): e.g., "FinTech", "Healthcare", "E-commerce"

**Why specify these?**
- AI tailors content to your target role
- Adds relevant industry keywords
- Highlights transferable skills
- Optimizes for ATS systems in that field

**What AI does during generation (10-15 seconds):**
1. **Action Verbs**: Replaces weak verbs with strong ones
   - ‚ùå "Responsible for managing team" 
   - ‚úÖ "Led cross-functional team of 8 engineers"
   
2. **Quantification**: Adds numbers and metrics where possible
   - ‚ùå "Improved system performance"
   - ‚úÖ "Optimized system performance by 45%, reducing load time from 3s to 1.5s"
   
3. **Impact Focus**: Shifts from responsibilities to achievements
   - ‚ùå "Managed social media accounts"
   - ‚úÖ "Grew social media following by 200% in 6 months, increasing engagement rate from 2% to 8%"
   
4. **Keyword Optimization**: Includes industry-relevant terms
   - For tech roles: specific technologies, frameworks, methodologies
   - For business roles: metrics, business outcomes, leadership terms
   
5. **Professional Polish**: Improves grammar, structure, and tone

**Your role:**
- Click "Generate with AI"
- Wait for the magic to happen
- Review the results in the next step

### Step 5: Review & Edit Your Resume

**What you'll see:**
- Full preview of your generated resume
- Professional formatting applied

**Two modes:**

#### View Mode (default)

#### Edit Mode

- Adjust wording as needed

2. **Summary**: Captures your essence professionally
3. **Experience**:

**Pro tip:** Read it out loud - if anything sounds awkward, edit it!

**When satisfied:**

## Tips for Best Results

### Content Quality

1. **Quantify Everything Possible**
   - Use numbers, percentages, dollar amounts
   - Show scale: team size, user base, budget
   - Demonstrate impact: growth, savings, efficiency

2. **Use Strong Action Verbs**
   - Led, Developed, Implemented, Launched
   - Increased, Optimized, Streamlined, Transformed
   - Avoid: "Responsible for", "Helped with", "Worked on"

3. **Show Impact, Not Just Responsibilities**
   - Focus on what you achieved, not what you did
   - Connect actions to business outcomes
   - Demonstrate value you brought

4. **Keep It Concise**
   - 1-2 pages maximum
   - Bullet points should be 1-2 lines
   - Remove redundant information

### Formatting

1. **Consistency Is Key**
   - Same date format throughout
   - Consistent bullet style
   - Uniform spacing

2. **Make It Scannable**
   - Use clear section headers
   - Bold important information
   - White space is your friend

3. **ATS-Friendly Practices**
   - Stick to standard fonts
   - Use standard section names
   - Avoid tables, graphics in main content
   - Include keywords from job descriptions

### Content Strategy

1. **Tailor to Each Application**
   - Emphasize relevant experience
   - Reorder bullets by relevance
   - Adjust skills section
   - Modify summary

2. **Prioritize Recent & Relevant**
   - More detail on recent roles
   - Briefly mention older positions
   - Focus on transferable skills

3. **Show Career Progression**
   - Demonstrate growth
   - Highlight promotions
   - Show increasing responsibility

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How long does the entire process take?**
A: Typically 10-20 minutes for a complete resume, depending on how much information you're starting with.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. The current version doesn't store your information permanently. Data is only held in memory during your session.

**Q: Can I use this for free?**
A: The application is free to use. However, you (or whoever set it up) need an Anthropic API key, which has costs based on usage.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No! This version works without accounts. Just start building.

### Resume Content

**Q: How long should my resume be?**
A: Aim for 1-2 pages. One page if you have less than 5 years of experience, two pages for more extensive backgrounds.

**Q: Should I include a photo?**
A: In the US, generally no. In some countries (Europe, Asia), it's common. Check local customs.

**Q: What if I don't have quantifiable achievements?**
A: Ask the AI Career Coach! It can help you identify metrics you might have overlooked or suggest ways to describe impact.

**Q: Should I include references?**
A: No. "References available upon request" is also unnecessary. Use that space for content.

### Technical Issues

**Q: My uploaded resume wasn't parsed correctly. What should I do?**
A: Try the "Start From Scratch" option and manually enter your information. The AI will still enhance it!

**Q: The AI is taking a long time to respond. Is something wrong?**
A: AI processing can take 10-30 seconds for complex operations. If it takes longer than a minute, refresh and try again.

**Q: Can I go back and change my layout choice?**
A: Currently, you'd need to start over. Save your resume data and it'll be quick to rebuild with a new layout.

**Q: The exported PDF doesn't look quite right. What should I do?**
A: Download the text version and copy it into your preferred document editor for final formatting touches.

### AI Features

**Q: How does the AI know what to recommend?**
A: The AI analyzes your experience level, industry, skills, and target role against thousands of successful resumes to provide tailored advice.

**Q: Will the AI write my resume for me?**
A: The AI enhances and optimizes your content, but it needs your real information and experiences. It makes what you provide better.

**Q: What if I disagree with the AI's suggestions?**
A: You have full control! The AI makes recommendations, but you make the final decisions. Edit anything you want.

**Q: Can the AI help with cover letters?**
A: Not in the current version, but ask the Career Coach for advice on how to approach cover letters for your situation!

### Best Practices

**Q: How often should I update my resume?**
A: Keep a master version and update it every 6 months or whenever you complete major achievements.

**Q: Should I have different versions for different jobs?**
A: Yes! Tailor your resume for each application. Save different versions for different types of roles.

**Q: What file format should I submit?**
A: Always PDF unless specifically asked for Word. PDFs preserve formatting across all systems.

**Q: How do I handle career gaps?**
A: Be honest. Use a brief explanation if needed, or ask the AI Career Coach for advice on positioning.

### Advanced Usage

**Q: Can I customize the templates?**
A: Currently, you choose from preset templates. Export to Word/text for further customization.

**Q: Can I save my resume and come back later?**
A: Not in the current version. Complete your resume in one session and save the exported file.

**Q: Can multiple people work on one resume?**
A: Currently no. But you can share exported files for collaboration in document editors.
